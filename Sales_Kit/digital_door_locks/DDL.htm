 DIGITAL DOOR LOCKS

Mortise Type

  • With lever handles and 5 mechanical overriding keys
  • Ideal for new or main doors
  • For replacement of old/faulty locks

 

Deadbolt Type

  • Additional or add-on lock with installation above or below existing door lock
  • Ideal for doors with long pull bars
  • For replacement of existing deadbolt locks
  • No removal of existing lock, hence no ugly hole patching

 

RIM Type

  • Additional or add-on lock with installation above or below existing door lock
  • Ideal for doors with long pull bars
  • For replacement of existing deadbolt locks
  • No removal of existing lock, hence no ugly hole patching
