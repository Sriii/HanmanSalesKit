SHT - 5181

Brochure User Manual Installation Manual Wiring Diagram Gallery


Description:

 

The Samsung Door Entry Phone (SHT-5181) is a multiplex door phone which is installed on the entrance wall of an apartment or a housing complex and is connected to each household through a camera.

SHT-5181 is an access control product for home safety and security to which an angle-adjustable high-pixel CCD camera is attached. The product enables checking the image of visitors outside the front door and to call each household from outside the house.

The product is designed to match the urban structures and apartment walls, and is made of aluminum to enhance durability.

.

 Features:

    • Door access control
    • (User access code&RFID card)
    • Call to households
    • Visitor's image save (SHN-5180)
Applications:
  • apartments - connected to the intercom system of your building
  • Residential
